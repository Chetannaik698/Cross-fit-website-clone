DO I HAVE TO BE FIT TO START CROSSFIT

No, you do not need to be fit to start CrossFit. You do CrossFit to become fit; you don’t become fit to do CrossFit.The obvious answer to these questions is “no.”

HOW WILL MY BODY CHANGE WITH REGULAR CROSSFIT TRAINING

Depending on your goals, CrossFit training and nutrition habits can be adjusted to produce a variety of changes to your body — all of which are beneficial.
